About us!!

Rahaa House (Rahaa Associates) is a Chennai-based, ISO 9001:2015 certified real estate developer specializing in land development, layout promotion, and property consulting, serving clients with high standards of transparency and professionalism for over 14 years


Rahaa House operates out of a spacious office in Chennai’s Maduravoyal area and is renowned for completing over 10 residential townships and 100+ layouts, delivering more than 25,000 plots to 25,000+ satisfied customers. The group prides itself on serving a wide segment of people, from government servants to business professionals, and has satisfied clients from abroad and various states across India.
